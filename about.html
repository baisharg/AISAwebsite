About AISAI

Understanding our mission, principles, and structure.

Mission & Approach

AISAI is exclusively dedicated to AI Safety, driven by the conviction that it is an urgent global priority. Our mission is to foster a cooperative community of researchers and professionals in Argentina, strengthening AI Safety development through collaboration and knowledge sharing. We believe Argentina holds significant potential for contributions, thanks to committed, bilingual individuals from strong academic backgrounds.

We operate under a horizontal organizational model, emphasizing collaborative decision-making, transparency, and openness to diverse ideas within the AI Safety field. Our direction is defined collectively, independent of specific political or institutional interests. AISAI exists for its members, ensuring initiatives arise organically from the community it serves.

Our Principles

Collaboration & Community

Fostering cooperation to strengthen AI Safety development and build a supportive network.

Independence & Autonomy

Operating free from external political or institutional pressures, guided solely by our members and mission.

AI Safety Focus

Exclusively dedicated to addressing AI Safety challenges, while valuing diverse perspectives within the field.

Multidisciplinarity

Welcoming contributions from all fields of knowledge, recognizing the complex nature of AI Safety.

Honesty & Transparency

Committing to open and clear communication internally and externally.

Good Faith & Mutual Trust

Operating with genuine collaboration, respect, and shared confidence in each other's integrity.

Scientific Rigor

Striving for high standards in research while remaining open to constructive criticism and learning.

International Integration

Engaging with the global AI Safety community to exchange knowledge and perspectives.

Founding Organizations

FAIR (Frontier Artificial Intelligence Research)

FAIR focuses on AI Safety as a sociotechnical challenge, developing techniques for preventing misaligned behavior, evaluating AI systems, and building societal confidence. It operates as an independent unit within UBA IALAB.

Visit FAIR Website

BAISH (Buenos Aires AI Safety Hub)

BAISH's mission is to support students entering the AI Safety field and conducting research, organizing activities like courses, discussion groups, and reading clubs to foster the community.
