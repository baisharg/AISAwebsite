Our Mission

To foster a cooperative community of researchers and professionals working together to strengthen the development of AI Safety in Argentina. We believe Argentina has significant potential to contribute to the field, thanks to committed, bilingual individuals with strong academic backgrounds. AISAI is exclusively dedicated to AI Safety, united by the conviction that it is an urgent global priority.
